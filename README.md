# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Title
Sales Performance Dashboard – Data Visualization & Business Insights

---

## 📖 Description
The **Sales Performance Dashboard** provides an interactive overview of business sales performance across different dimensions such as **time, country, product, and customer segments**.  

It helps users quickly analyze key business metrics including **total sales, units sold, and profit**, while identifying trends and performance patterns. The dashboard enables decision-makers to gain insights into which **products, regions, and segments contribute the most to revenue and profit**.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Power BI** – Data visualization and dashboard creation  
- **Microsoft Excel / CSV** – Data preprocessing  
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Calculations and metrics  
- **Data Modeling** – Structuring and relationships between fields  

---

## 📂 Data Source
- Dataset obtained from **Kaggle**
- Contains historical sales records including:
  - Sales amount
  - Units sold
  - Profit
  - Product details
  - Country information
  - Customer segments
  - Discount bands
  - Year and month

---

## ⚙️ Features
- 📊 **KPI Cards**
  - Total Sales
  - Total Units Sold
  - Total Profit

- 📈 **Sales Trend Analysis**
  - Monthly sales trend visualization

- 🧩 **Segment Analysis**
  - Sales distribution across different customer segments

- 📦 **Product Performance**
  - Profit comparison across different products

- 🌍 **Geographical Insights**
  - Profit distribution by country

- 🏷 **Discount Impact Analysis**
  - Sales comparison based on discount bands

- 🎛 **Interactive Filters**
  - Year selection
  - Country filtering
  - Product filtering

---

## ⭐ Highlights
- Clean and **dark-themed professional dashboard design**
- **Interactive filtering** for dynamic data exploration
- Clear visualization of **sales trends and profitability**
- Helps identify:
  - Top-performing products
  - Most profitable countries
